A Web-Radio ?

We had to do a Web-Radio ! From the recording to the broadcast

A screen of the app

What did we check ?

We checked a lot of radios, from NRJ to RFM, we studied it thouroughly, what could we do, to see if we could add it in our site.

The problem we had, was that radios are not recording with their site, so, we had to work from scratch there.

For this, we used PHP, HTML/CSS with TWIG, Eloquent, and JavaScript with jQuery.

The Back-End

As of now, the back-end is storing the programs, the users, in a database. It is also concatening the multiple audio tracks we have together.

The Front-End

The front-end is formatting the data we're sending him from the back-end, adding some style to it, to make it pretty and easier to read.
