Bon-Duelle

Bon-duelle is a little battle game we had to do in a PHP course, it was a project that lasted two months.

Get it on GitLab

A character we used in our game

What did we use ?

We were four to do this, and we used PHP, with Eloquent, to communicate with our Database, to help with the different pages.

We had two parts in our game, the administrator part, and the user one, the staff can manage the different accounts, and the many characters we have. He can even add some if he wants.

The user, when he's logged in, can play a game, in 1v1, one versus one, or 3v3, where he's choosing for both sides. When he clicks on play, he can choose to defend or attack for his side. The ennemies are playing by themselves.

Eloquent

Eloquent is one of the new technology we discovered during this project, making it so much easier. The SQL request we did were not written by our group, but by Eloquent itself. The only problem is that we cannot see what he is doing, or how much request he si actually doing.

TWIG

Twig is also a new technology, making the HTML pages so much faster to do, as we could make templates are use them again and again in all of our pages, like the navbar, instead of copy/pasting it in each page.
